Coalinga High School is a public high school in Coalinga, California. It lies southwest of Fresno, California and northeast of San Luis Obispo, California. It serves both the communities of Coalinga and Huron. Its mascot is the Horned Toad.

  • Mascot: The Horned Toad
  • Founded: 1909
  • Colors: Black, Orange
  • District: Coalinga-Huron Joint Unified School District
  • Motto: Once a Toad, Always a Toad
